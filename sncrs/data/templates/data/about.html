About

SNCRS is the brain-child of the infamous Colton Lewis. Through sheer force of will, Colton herded his Saint Louis crew of cats into regular SmashNights. As time progessed, Colton poured hours upon hours into maintaining a scoring system designed to keep attendees competitive and interested. The original iterations were glorious, but required a great deal of work to maintain. To ease the burden, we have developed this flexible system for maintaining smash data and automations to pull and process data. We hope this tool will benefit other intrepid Smashers, allowing them to maintain the same type of system we have grown to love.
